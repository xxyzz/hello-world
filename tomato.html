This Pomodoro timer's default work timer is twenty-five minutes, short break timer is five minutes. After four pomodoros, you will have a fifteen minutes rest. Feel free to alter the default settings!

You can also press the space key to start/pause/resume the timer or press the key R to reset the timer.

And welcome to visit my blog.
